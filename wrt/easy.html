My thoughts on EasyOS

i'm still using 6.0. it is very nice, easy to understand and i like it just about the same as linux mint, a little more. it fits entirely on my usb stick and runs well on any pc! it's not the ideal, since it's oversimplified and doesn't have a decent package manager, but i think it would've been a better start than ubuntu..

the use of open-source programs is good, i hope it stays that way. from what i can tell from the developer's page, it is just a passion project that he's sharing.

EasyOS feels familiar, but also like it's own thing. it's good.
