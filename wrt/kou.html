You open your eyes and find yourself standing in an empty parking lot that seems to stretch endlessly. There are no buildings, nor any cars to be seen. The only other things you see are the clouds in the sky and a shopping cart next to you. It goes to a store you can't decipher the name of.

You can feel, almost hear a sort of obligation to take it to wherever that store is. You grab the handle and roll it in a direction. The empty parking lot continues for what must be miles. Who designed this horrible place.

After about three days' worth of pushing the cart, you do find a few cars. The buildings still cannot be seen. The symbols on the cart have changed, and their colours are foriegn— nay, their colours should not be real.
